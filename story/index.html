This is my story

Hi, my name is Fiona. I am a cat, obviously. I've had a bit of a storied life. I don't remember much about the younger years. But my story really starts at 3 in the morning ourside of the Westchester Humane Society about 4 years ago. I was left in a box. No information, no contact, nothing. It's OK, I don't mind that much what happened to me, because at least I was left with people who took me in and helped me.

I had some trouble adjusting to life at the shelter, and I got into a lot of fights with the other cats. They put me in my own cage (which was a much better deal if you ask me, more privacy and befitting a queen such as myself!). One day these people came in to look at me. I knew I had them the minute they laid eyes on me. They were powerless to resist me and I knew they would take me home. They did, of course, and it's been me ruling their lives ever since.

Anyway, this is my website, feel free to look around and check out my pictures, follow me on twitter or instagram, and find out more about where I came from and how you can get involved with shelter pets.
