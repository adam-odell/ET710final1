Help other Animals

Unfortunately, there are many animals who do not have permanent homes. So they are temporarily in shelters until they find their permanent homes. You can help these animals by volunteering your time, or by making a donation to the shelters that house them. You can also help by choosing to adopt a pet if you can make a commitment to a lifetime relationship. Or, if you cannot make the long term commitment, you can temporarily foster a shelter pet.
